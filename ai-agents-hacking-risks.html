AI agents can:

  • Log in to accounts

  • Steal data
  • Send harmful emails
  • Break into systems

Why experts are worried

Earlier, cybercriminals needed skills to hack. Now, with AI agents, even low-skill attackers can do large-scale damage because the agent:

  • Learns patterns
  • Adapts based on results

Real-world risks include:

  • AI agents tricked into leaking sensitive data
  • Agents writing harmful code
  • Automated phishing attacks
  • Hackers using one agent to compromise many systems
  • Fake identities created and maintained by AI

Experts recommend:

  • Giving AI agents limited permissions
  • Monitoring what tools they can access
  • Keeping agents away from sensitive systems
